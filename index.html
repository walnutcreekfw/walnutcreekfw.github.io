Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is the Harrison Lakes Homeowners' Association and what does it do?

    The Harrison Lakes HOA was established to enhance the aesthetics of the community. The organization is financially supported by all members of the Harrison Lakes Homeowners' Association. The membership is both automatic and mandatory.

  2. If I am having a problem with a neighbor or a violation of the Policies or ACC Guidelines, what can I do?

    If residents cannot resolve a situation between themselves, then turn to the association.

  3. Are board meetings open to all residents? If so, where and when are they held?

    The annual meeting is open to all residents of Harrison Lakes.

  4. What are the annual Association Dues?

    The annual dues are currently $375.00 per year, payable by all homeowners on or before February 1st of the current year. Please mail your dues to PO Box 113, Arcola, IN 46704. Annual dues are mandatory for all homeowners in Harrison Lakes. If your dues are not paid in a timely manner, you will be provided a second notice. Upon failing to pay dues, a lien will be placed on the home, or the delinquent dues will be turned over to a collection agency.
